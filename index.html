Creator Profile

Meet the Developer // Lance

About Me

Hi! I'm Lance. I'm originally from the busy streets of Pasig, but I currently live out in Magallanes, Cavite and study at De Guia Academy.

Why I Built This Website

I created this project to be my personal digital time capsule. Life moves fast, and it's easy to lose track of the little things—the late-night thoughts, the small milestones, and the exact feelings you have on a random day. I wanted a private, secure corner of the internet to store my memories so they never fade away.

The Story Behind "Cece"

While this site is a place for my own memories, the heart behind it belongs to someone incredibly special who is a beautiful mix of Mestiza and Chinita. She goes by a couple of nicknames (like Chin), but I call her 'Cece'—mostly because whenever she laughs, her eyes literally turn into two perfect, little inverted C's. Naming this app Project Cece is just my quiet, romantic way of keeping her close and saying hi through the code.
