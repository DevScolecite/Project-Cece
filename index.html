Project Information

Project Cece is my personal, lightweight diary companion. I built it to have a clean, distraction-free space to structure my everyday thoughts, clear my mind, and log moments that matter before they fade. Because some memories need to stay entirely private, I engineered a secure PIN gate wrapper to keep my vault safe from prying eyes. Plus, to keep the workspace inspiring, the interface is completely fluid—allowing me to swap styling and colorways layout by layout to perfectly match whatever vibe or mood I’m writing in.
